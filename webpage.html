Some facts about parrot :

My fav bird is Parrot. Parrots are capable of associating words with objects and situations, and can even use tools and solve problems. Some scientists argue that they have an IQ equivalent to that of a four-year-old. Parrots are also quite playful. One thing that makes parrots pretty popular pets is their ability to learn and imitate sounds.
